how it works

Apply in minutes

1. Submit your application

Fill out your company information on our online form.

Am I eligible?

Eligibility is based on where you live not where you're from.In general, we do not provide services to customers who live in the following regions:

Belarus, Burundi, Central African Republic, Cuba, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Iran, Iraq, Lebanon, Libya, Nicaragua, North Korea, Somalia, South Sudan, Sudan, Syria, Ukraine, Venezuela, Yemen, Zimbabwe

2. We file for you

We'll file the paperwork for your company and file your EIN number on your behalf.

How long will it take to file?

For applicants with a Social Security Number (SSN), the entire process takes around 5-7 business days. For non-residents, the process can take up to 3 weeks as filing for non-resident EINs can take up to 14 business days.

3. Get your business documents

We deliver your business documents, including your business formation certificate and employer identification number (EIN), straight to your personalized dashboard.

4. We open your bank account

We'll setup your bank account and hand it off to you so you can get started on your business.

What banking service do you use?

We partner with Mercury Bank, a fully licensed and FDIC insured bank. They’re primarily an online bank with full digital access via a computer.

FAQS

Find answers to frequently asked questions

Do I need to visit the USA to open a bank account?
+
Nope! We handle everything remotely. You'll just need to provide your passport as identity verification and we'll work directly with the bank to open your account.
Do you help with taxes?
+
We work with our partners to provide you with an easy way to file your taxes. Our partner Bookmate has packages based on revenue and can file for any type of entity that you can file with VCfilling. VCfilling customers have access to an exclusive 10% discount on all Bookmate services. Federal tax returns are due in March or April depending on the entity. Schedule a free consultation with Bookmate on their website at https://www.trybookmate.co/contactus
What do I need to get started?
+
To get started, simply fill out our online form, submit your payment, upload your documentation and we'll handle the rest.​
What is the difference between using you and an expensive lawyer?
+
Nothing. We offer more than a lawyer can at much lower costs because we use technology and automation to reduce administrative costs. Worried about accuracy? Our processes are vetted by lawyers for validity to ensure we're filing your information correctly.
Do I need to be a U.S. Citizen with an SSN to use VCfilling?
+
No, you don't need to be a U.S. Citizen with an SSN to use VCfilling. We support non-residents from over 175+ countries. If you do have an SSN, let us know and we can expedite your application.
Why should I start a U.S. Company?
+
Most of our clients start U.S. companies to access the U.S. market, reduce their personal liability, and access U.S. payment/banking infrastructure.
